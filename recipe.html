Chef Ama's
Jollof Rice Recipe

By: Agbasielo Amarachi

About Ama's Recipe:

If there's a food I can eat the rest of my life it is definitely going to be jollof rice. Jollof rice is one of the most common if not the most common West Africa rice dishes which when prepared well is always finger-licking good and gives an award winning taste and satifasction.

Here is a short recipe on how I make mine

Firstly, here are the ingredients a good jollof rice calls for:

  • Groundnut oil
  • 1/2 of a big bulb onion
  • A medium tin tomato or fresh tomatoor even both (your choice)
  • Seasonings (maggi,salt,curry,bay leaves, and others of your choosing)
  • Rice of course
  • Any meat of your choice

How to prepare it:

  1. Parboil rice, wash and set aside
  2. Sautee onions for 3/5 minutes then add ginger,garlicand grounded fresh pepper. Fry this for some minutes
  3. Add the tomato and fry for few minutes
  4. Add the Seasonings (salt, maggi and curry etc)and bay leaves as well and fry some more until a little dry
  5. add the chicken stock (water used to boil the chicken/meat)
  6. add the washed rice
  7. once the rice comes to a boil, low the heat of the gas
  8. cover with foil paper and leave to cook.
  9. when ready serve with nice dodo by the side and fried chicken/turkey/meat/fish just like the beautiful one above
  10. Enjoy!!
